NYS licensed Home Health Care Agencies serving the NYC five boroughs, Nassau County, Westchester currently hiring bilingual (Chinese English) Webmaster to meet the needs of its business growth and Offices expansions.

```Duties```
- Develop and maintain websites, ensuring functionality, accessibility, and user experience
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to gather requirements and implement web solutions
- Create and optimize web content, including text, images, and multimedia elements
- Monitor website performance, identify issues, and implement necessary fixes or improvements
- Conduct regular audits to ensure compliance with web standards and best practices
- Implement SEO strategies to improve website visibility and search engine rankings
- Manage web hosting and domain registration activities
- Stay up-to-date with emerging web technologies and industry trends

```Requirements```
- Proven experience as a Webmaster or similar role
- Strong knowledge of system architecture and web development principles
- Proficiency in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, TypeScript, and other relevant programming languages
- Familiarity with content management systems (CMS) and website builders
- Experience with cloud platforms such as AWS or Azure
- Knowledge of .NET framework and related technologies
- Familiarity with DevOps tools like Jenkins or Ansible
- Understanding of SQL databases and data management principles
- Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail
